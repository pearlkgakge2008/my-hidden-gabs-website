This weeks Top picks!

Here are some spots to check out

Two Six Seven

Two Six Seven is a relaxed, social restaurant and bar at Riverwalk in Gaborone where people usually go to unwind, eat, and hang out with friends. The atmosphere is casual but slightly stylish, with warm lighting and an easy-going crowd that often starts with dinner and flows into drinks later in the evening.

Cappucinos

Cappuccinos is located in Airport Junction. It has a relaxed, easy mall café vibe. The atmosphere feels warm with chatter of conversations from families, friends, and people just passing time over food, coffee or drinks.

Rhapsodys

Is a resturant located in Sebele. A great spot for social hangouts. During the day its calm and the energy is light, but at night it becomes more lively with music, cocktails and social crowds.
